# **App Name**: Mood Oil Navigator

## Core Features:

- Start Screen: Interactive start screen with a clear call to action: 'Find Your Oil'.
- Mood Quiz: A series of 5 two-option questions related to the user's mood, preferences, and lifestyle to narrow down oil recommendations.
- Oil Recommendation: AI-powered oil suggestion tool: Based on the user's answers, recommend the most suitable oil from a predefined set of oils. The AI tool considers the emotional categories of happiness, anxiety, depression, fatigue, irritation and stress, and the corresponding selection.
- Oil Presentation: Display the recommended oil with its name and a brief description of its benefits, matching the oil to the user's answers in the mood quiz.
- Oil Details: A detailed description page for each oil, including an image, a comprehensive overview of its properties, and potential uses.
- Purchase Link: Links to purchase the recommended oil for ease of access and convenience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft orange (#FFB347) to evoke warmth and happiness, aligning with the aromatherapy context.
- Background color: Pale green (#E8F5E9) for a refreshing and natural feel.
- Accent color: Muted yellow (#FFDA63) to add a touch of vibrancy and balance.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif font, provides an elegant, intellectual and contemporary feel that fits the personal reflection sought when considering one's mood.
- Use simple, line-based icons for mood categories and oil properties, ensuring clarity and ease of understanding.
- Maintain a clean and minimalist layout with ample spacing, allowing users to focus on the content without feeling overwhelmed.
- Implement subtle transitions and animations when navigating between questions and displaying results, providing a smooth and engaging user experience.
